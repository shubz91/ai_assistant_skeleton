## Usage Rules

1. **Backgrounds**: Page background is never pure white — always use a warm or light tinted value
2. **Structural borders**: Major dividers use 2px foreground color; internal use 1px divider color
3. **Single accent rule**: Maximum one accent color per composition
4. **Body text**: Never use pure black for body copy — only for headlines and borders
5. **Contrast**: All text must meet WCAG AA contrast requirements (4.5:1 normal, 3:1 large)
