- **Footer**: Logo with tagline

## Usage Rules

1. **Approved colors only** — Do not recolor the logo
2. **Appropriate backgrounds** — Ensure sufficient contrast
3. **Clearspace** — Maintain minimum clearspace on all sides
4. **Minimum size** — Full logo: 100px width on screen
5. **Do not** alter proportions, rotate, or add effects
